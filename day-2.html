19 April 2026 · Day 2

Day 2 — hard work and fun

A busy and energetic second day, from a late breakfast of waffles to a foosball battle and crossfit session at dusk, with hours of focused hackathon work in between.

10:30

Late breakfast — waffles saved the day

We arrived a little late for breakfast, but thankfully there were still waffles, snacks, and coffee waiting for us. A much-needed start before diving back into the project.

11:00

Resuming work & refining our direction

We picked up where we had left off the night before, reviewing our group brainstorm and adding a few new ideas that had come to us overnight. By the end of the morning, we had a clearer direction and had started to finalise the approach we wanted to take for the project.

12:00

Lunch — pasta, chicken & more coffee

We kept pushing forward on the solution through the morning, doing our best to stay focused even as the catering team set up the lunch table right beside us. Eventually we were called over — pasta with chicken, followed by, of course, more coffee.

14:00

Foosball — a friendly 2v2

After lunch we played a 2v2 foosball battle with some teammates. Fortunately one of us had brought a ball from home, as there wasn't one on site otherwise. After the fun and games, it was straight back to work.

14:30

Back to the challenge — food stock in a crisis

We continued developing our idea around creating a more efficient way to manage food stock during a crisis situation. The work was progressing well and the team was staying aligned on the direction.

16:00

Crossfit session

The organisers had planned a crossfit session and we had been told in advance to bring sports clothes. It was a simple but fun activity, even if some of the instructions were hard to follow at times — especially with the French explanations. Typical Belgian rain decided to join us mid-session, but it only added to the experience. The session helped us reset, stretch out, and feel re-energised before returning to the challenge.

17:00

Final push on the project

With fresh energy after the crossfit break, we dove back into the project and kept working on the problem of how to efficiently distribute food stock in a crisis situation. It was a productive evening session.

19:30

Dinner & train home

We ended the day with dinner and then headed home by train. The journey back was peaceful — most of us fell asleep almost immediately after a long and busy day.
